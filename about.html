about T-babs Technology Institute

T-babs Technology Institute is a collaborative learning center built by engineers, for engineers. We believe in knowledge transfer and hands-on mastery. Our approach combines industry-led workshops, real-world projects, and a vibrant community — all under the Learning center umbrella.

What started as small study circles has grown into a hub where technologists upskill, research, and mentor. We are proudly “by T-babs Technology Institute, for T-babs Technology Institute”.
